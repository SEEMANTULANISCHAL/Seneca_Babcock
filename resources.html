Baby & Children's Ministry

Harvest House of South Buffalo

By Appointment

Mothers, fathers, grandparents, and other caregivers can "shop" free of charge for items their children need. Each child receives a week's worth of clothing at no cost to their family/caregiver.

Location:
Harvest House
175 Jefferson Avenue
Buffalo, NY 14210
Phone:
(716) 824-7818 ext. 214
Hours:
Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
(By Appointment Only)
Saturday - Sunday: Closed
Email:
babyministry@harvesthousebuffalo.org

Items Available

  • Children's clothing (all sizes)
  • Safe cribs and car seats
  • Strollers and pack 'n plays
  • Toys, books, and pajamas
  • Socks and underwear

Requirements

Referral form required from agencies like Catholic Charities, WIC, or Buffalo Prenatal. Must bring ID for parent/caregiver and children. Call or email to schedule appointment.

Free Families Children Appointment Required Referral Needed

Child Care Subsidy Program

New York State Office of Children and Family Services

Available Now

Financial assistance program to help eligible families pay for child care services while working, attending school, or in training programs.

Service Area:
Buffalo, NY 14210
Contact:
NYS OCFS Buffalo Office
Website:
ocfs.ny.gov

Eligibility Requirements

  • Income must meet state guidelines
  • Working, in school, or training
  • Child must be under 13 (or under 19 with special needs)
  • New York State resident

How to Apply

Contact your local Department of Social Services or apply online through the NYS OCFS website. Documentation of income, employment, and child's information required.

Financial Assistance Child Care Working Families State Program

Disability Benefits Information

Buffalo Area Resources

Available Now

Information and assistance with disability benefits applications, including Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) and Supplemental Security Income (SSI).

Service Area:
Buffalo, NY 14210
SSA National:
1-800-772-1213
Website:
ssa.gov

Benefits Available

  • Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Medicare benefits
  • Medicaid assistance
  • Vocational rehabilitation

How to Apply

Apply online at ssa.gov, call the national number, or visit the local Social Security office. Gather medical records, work history, and personal documentation before applying.
